Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Department at Dubai Municipality. The department was established through a decree (Law No. 6, 2001) of the Ruler of Dubai, the late Sheikh Maktoum Bin Rashid Al Maktoum. The law stipulated that the GIS Department is considered to be the only official body which allows producing geographical data in the Emirate.
GIS Department is considered as an official portal to provide geographical data services, images and spatial maps of Dubai to governmental bodies, the private sector and individuals.

Our Vision

Provide integrated Geospatial information of the Emirate of Dubai and enable everyone to utilize it.

Our Mission

Enable users to utilize spatial information in different fields such as environment, health, economy, planning and public services..etc through applications and interactive maps.
